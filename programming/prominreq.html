Programmer minimum requirement

Time: 7/18/2021
By Setiawan Jodi

Programmer minimum requirement:

  • Know basic computer usage
  • Know some English
  • And some time and practice

You don't need any talent to start being a programmer. What is needed is curiosity and perseverance. also, you don't need a tutoring program (I still recommend it) because the programming language developed in the internet era so all documentation and how-to are on the internet.

If you encounter problems don't be afraid to google the problems you encounter or ask other programmers.

To get started as a programmer, you don't need any special software or computer. a normal computer will do its job, but a modern computer with more than 8 GB of ram is recommended.

Study material recommendations:

  • https://www.w3schools.com/
  • https://developer.mozilla.org/ (If you want some advanced documentation)
  • https://www.sololearn.com/ (For bite-size programming tutorial)
  • https://stackoverflow.com/ (A great place to ask and how to solve problems)

Recommended development software:

  • https://atom.io/ (Great IDE for HTML language)
  • https://code.visualstudio.com/ (Great IDE for multiple programming languages)
  • https://www.jetbrains.com/pycharm/ (IDE for Python language)
  • https://www.apachefriends.org/ (For testing your website)

Benefit being a programmer:

  • The salary is good (about $86,550)1
  • Increased typing speed
  • Less likely get computer virus or getting scam2
  • You can work from home3
  • Become a much better netizen (internet citizen)
  • You can have more than one specialty (like becoming a data scientist, sysadmin, computer engineer, and more)
  • And many more
Footnote
1 Your salary is dependant on your experience, region, type of task, and many more. Salary data is from https://money.usnews.com/
2 For certain people. You can do it if you are careful on the internet
3 Dependant on your type of task
